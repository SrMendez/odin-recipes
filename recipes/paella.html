Paella Valenciana Recipe

There's only one Paella Valenciana, and it comes from Valencia.

Over time different kinds of rices were created by chinging diferent or all the ingredients.

Traditionally it was made with ten basic ingredients that people could afford. That's the authentic paella valenciana

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Chicken
  • Rabbit
  • Green beans "Ferraura"
  • Carob beans "Garrof√≥"
  • Tomatoes
  • Bottled or hard water
  • Salt
  • Saffron
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Put a nice amount of olive oil in the pan and level it off
  2. When it's hot add a little salt and then add the chicken and rabbit.
  3. Once the meat is browned, move it outside the paella and add the cut green and carob beans. Saute for 2-3 minutes without burning it.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoe and fry it.
  5. Pour the water until it covers the rivets on the handles and let it cook.
  6. Turn up the heat and cook everything for about 5-10 minutes. Add the saffron and salt.
  7. Before adding the rice, test the salt level and if it's a little salty and the water is boiling, add the rice.
  8. There are two ways to measure the amount of rice. The first one you have to make a cross with the rice, sitcking out a finger of the broth. The second one, you will have to trust the typical measure, double and a half of water for one of rice.
  9. Leave the fire at maximum for 5 minutes and then go down to low-medium heat. Cook for about 18 minutes in total, no more or you will pass the rice.
