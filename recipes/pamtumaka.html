Pà amb tomàquet

Bread with Tomato Recipe

One of the most typical dish in Catalonia and also in Spain.

It is a quick, simple and finger licking recipe.

There are many variations and we will see some of them.

Ingredients

  • Slices of bread
  • Tomatoes on the vine
  • Some extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt (optional)
  • 1 Garlic clove, peeled (optional)

Steps

  1. Toast the bread in a toaster or a pan.
  2. Cut the tomatoes in half and spred directly on the bread.
  3. If you want more taste of the tomatose, you could grate them using a grater, discarting the skin.
  4. Add a splash of oil and that's it!
  5. You cut eat the bread with tomatoe and some salt on top of it.
  6. You can also cut some cheese or some ham and put it on top aswell.
  7. Another variation is to cut the garlic in half lengthwise and spread it on the bread.
