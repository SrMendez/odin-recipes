Tortilla de Patatas Recipe

Potato omelette is one of the first recipes that every chef must know.

We are going to cook a typical spanish tortilla with two special touches.

We are going to cook the potatoes cold, starting with oil to obtain a confit that makes them creamier.

Secondly, we are going to caramelize the onion and add it to the potatoes.

Ingredients for 4 people

  • Potatoes 700g
  • Onions 300g
  • 6 eggs
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. We start caramelizing the onion. First, peel the onion and cut it into julienne strips.
  2. Then put it in a frying pan over a very low heat. We are interested in poaching it very slowly. The onion will caramelize on its own natural sugars.
  3. While the onion is being cooked, peel the potatoes and cut them into thin slices. Leave them in water for 15 minutes and put a pan with abundant olive oil on the fire.
  4. Add the potatoes and let them fry very slowly until they are candied.
  5. Remove the potatoes, drain them well and place them in a large bowl. Drain the onion aswell and put it on the potatoes.
  6. Beat the eggs and add them in to the bowl, stirring with a fork so that the three ingredients are well mixed.
  7. Curdle the tortilla in a pan with a tablespoon of oil for about three or four minutes and turn it over.
  8. If you are not very experienced, try turning it over with the help of a plate.
